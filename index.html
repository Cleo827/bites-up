Frequently Asked Questions

What is Delux Bite?

Delux Bite is your go-to destination for delicious meals delivered fast to your door in Jos, Bokkos, and Plateau State. We offer a variety of popular dishes and easy online ordering.

How do I place an order?

Click the "Order Now" button on the homepage or menu section, add items to your cart, and check out. You can also use the reservation feature for events.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash on delivery and bank transfer. Online payment options are now live.

Where do you deliver?

We deliver to Jos, Bokkos, and surrounding areas in Plateau State, Nigeria. Please provide a detailed address when ordering.

Can I make a reservation for an event?

Yes! Visit the Reservation section and click "Reserve Now" to book our beautiful ambience for your event.

How do I contact customer support?

You can reach us via the contact details at the bottom of the page, use the feedback form, or chat with a representative using the WhatsApp icon.

How do I give feedback?

Click the "Feedback" link in the navigation menu to access our feedback form and share your thoughts with us.

How can I add a profile picture?

Click on the avatar circle at the side of the page and select a picture from your gallery to upload as your profile picture.

How do I track my order?

After placing your order, you will receive a confirmation message. You can contact us via WhatsApp or phone to get real-time updates on your order status.

Can I cancel or change my order?

Yes, you can cancel or modify your order by contacting us immediately after placing it. Once your food is being prepared, changes may not be possible.

Do you offer vegetarian or vegan options?

Yes, we offer a variety of vegetarian and vegan dishes. Please check our menu or ask our staff for recommendations.

Is there a delivery fee?

How do I know my reservation is confirmed?

After submitting your reservation, you will receive a confirmation email or WhatsApp message from our team. Please check your inbox or messages.

Can I order for a group or event?

Absolutely! We cater for groups, parties, and events. Please use the reservation page or contact us directly for special arrangements and discounts.

Do you have special offers or discounts?

We run special offers from time to time. Subscribe to our newsletter or follow us on social media to stay updated on our latest deals.

How do I subscribe to the newsletter?

Scroll down to the newsletter section on our homepage, enter your email, and click "Subscribe" to receive updates and exclusive offers.

Is my personal information safe?

Yes, your privacy is important to us. We use secure systems to protect your data and never share your information with third parties.

Can I pay online?

Yes, online payment is available. You can also pay via bank transfer or cash on delivery.
